Mindmapping

I began by making a mind map in PowerPoint using one of their built-in SmartArt design charts. This would include both the brand and the product being sold - I chose a perfume because I find there's a lot more room for displaying a product and descriptive language.

Once done, I took the entire list and plugged it into chatgpt, asking for an image of the perfume, a description and a tagline.
